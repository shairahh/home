WHO IS THE WEEKND

E A R L Y   L I F E.

Abel was born on February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Ontario as the only child to Ethiopian immigrants Makkonen Tesfaye and Samrawit Hailu. However, when he was younger, his father had left him and his mother. He was then raised by his grandmother and mother in the district of Scarborough. As he was raised by his grandmother, he picked up his first language, Amharic, from her. The Weeknd attended both West Hill Collegiate Institute and Birchmount Collegiate Institute in high school. He had been kicked out from one and dropped out of the other in order to prioritize his party life and pursue music.

A W A R D S.

The Weeknd has won many awards for his outstanding talent! These include...

  • Top Artist, Top Hot 100 Artist, Top Hot 100 Song Presented by Rockstar, Top Male Artist, Top R&B Artist, Top R&B Male Artist, Top R&B Album
  • Five AMAS
  • The Guinness World record for most weeks in the top 5 of the US Hot 100 track with his number one hit, “Blinding Lights!”
  • GRAMMY Awards: He had won his first of three Grammy awards in 2015 for his album “Beauty Behind The Madness.” The official Grammy website, has more information on his nominations and wins.

R I S E   T O   F A M E.

Before Abel had dropped out of high school in 2010, he had begun his career by anonymously releasing his music to the internet through his YouTube channel and rose to fame shortly after. The Weeknd had been influenced greatly by his cultural music and Prince. Others include Michael Jackson, David Bowie (a huge inspiration for his 2016 album, “Starboy,”) and R. Kelly.
